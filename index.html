Catalog

In order for our app to function properly, there are a few steps to get started. First you need to catalog and organize which plants you’ll be watering with our software. This can easily be found in the “Search” or “Catalog” bar. Once you have your plants cataloged, our algorithm will take care of the rest! Once you set up each of your plants to your liking, you can completely customize how you’d like to be notified. On top of this, you can easily access a record that shows exactly when and how much you’ve been watering your plants.

Relax

Now that you have everything set up and good to go, kick your feet back and watch as the utility bill goes down and your plants get greener! Countless hours of googling information about your plants go out the window! You no longer have to stress about over watering your succulents or neglecting your hidden plants ever again.
