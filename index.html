Our Misson

In a time when there's an app for organizing just about every aspect of our lives, this isn't easlily available for plant care.

Water my Plants aims to help you get more organized with your plant care by keeping track of your polatns. We want to help you become a savvy plant parent and never again have to remember when you watered last.
